## Notes

1. **Salary data** reflects US national median from BLS OES (May 2024) and Glassdoor/Payscale where BLS does not have a direct match. Tech salaries skew higher in SF/NYC/Seattle (1.3-1.6x multiplier).
2. **Growth outlook** uses BLS 2023-2033 Employment Projections. "Rapid" = much faster than average (10%+). All tech categories assume continued AI-driven demand.
3. **Automation risk** follows Oxford/Frey-Osborne framework and McKinsey Global Institute assessments. Roles requiring creativity, complex judgment, and human interaction score "Low."
4. **Remote potential** reflects post-2024 norms. Many companies have shifted to hybrid; "Fully Remote" means the majority of roles in the field offer full remote.
5. **Entry-level ranges** represent 0-2 years experience. **Senior ranges** represent 8-15+ years or staff/principal level.
6. **Employment counts** are rounded from BLS OES. Some emerging roles (AI Engineer, Growth Hacker, ESG Analyst) use industry estimates since BLS does not track them separately.
7. **Investment Banker** and **Startup Founder** compensation is highly variable; base salary shown does not include bonuses/equity which can 2-5x total compensation.
8. **Solar/Wind Technician** has the highest projected growth rate of any career in this dataset at 45%, driven by the clean energy transition.
9. **Truck Driver** automation risk is rated "High" due to autonomous vehicle development, though full automation timeline remains uncertain (2030-2040+).
10. **Military Officer** salary reflects base pay; total compensation with BAH, BAS, and benefits is typically 1.5-2x base.
